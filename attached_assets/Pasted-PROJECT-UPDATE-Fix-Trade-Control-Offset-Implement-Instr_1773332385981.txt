PROJECT UPDATE: Fix Trade Control Offset & Implement Instrument-Aware PnL Engine

Context:
The platform currently applies Trade Control offsets relative to the live market price. When the market moves heavily against the trade, the offset is too small to overcome the loss.

Example:
XAU/USD BUY trade entry = 5513.92
Market price = 5324.08
Market moved -189.84
Admin sets 100 pip offset (~$1)
PnL still shows large loss.

We must change the Trade Control system so profit is calculated relative to the entry price, not the current market price.

---

GOAL

Ensure that when Trade Control is active:

PnL is determined by pip offset from entry price.

Example:

Entry = 5513.92
Target pips = 100
Lot = 0.11

User should see:

~+$11 profit

regardless of where the real market moved.

---

STEP 1 — CREATE PIP CALCULATOR UTILITY

Create file:

server/utils/pipCalculator.ts

Functions:

getPipSize(symbol)

Rules:

JPY pairs → 0.01
XAUUSD / XAGUSD → 0.01
Standard forex → 0.0001
Indices / stocks → 1

---

getPipValue(symbol)

Rules:

XAUUSD / XAGUSD → 1
Standard forex → 10
Indices / stocks → 1

---

calculatePnL(symbol, side, entryPrice, currentPrice, lotSize)

Algorithm:

pipSize = getPipSize(symbol)
pipValue = getPipValue(symbol)

priceDiff = currentPrice - entryPrice

if side == "sell":
priceDiff = entryPrice - currentPrice

pips = priceDiff / pipSize

pnl = pips * pipValue * lotSize

return pnl

---

STEP 2 — FIX TRADE CONTROL OFFSET LOGIC

File:

server/services/priceModifier.ts

Current behavior:
offset is applied relative to current market price.

Replace with:

offset applied relative to trade entry price.

Implementation:

accumulatedOffset += speedStep

For BUY:

effectivePrice = entryPrice + accumulatedOffset

For SELL:

effectivePrice = entryPrice - accumulatedOffset

Do not use raw market price when trade control is active.

---

STEP 3 — UPDATE PRICE ENGINE

File:

server/price-engine.ts

Function:

checkSlTpAndBroadcastPnl()

Replace inline formula:

(currentPrice - entryPrice) * units

with:

calculatePnL(symbol, side, entryPrice, effectivePrice, lotSize)

Import:

server/utils/pipCalculator.ts

---

STEP 4 — PASS ENTRY PRICE INTO PRICE MODIFIER

Modify tick() call so it receives:

entryPrice

instead of only raw market price.

Example:

tick(symbol, userId, entryPrice, rawMarketPrice)

---

STEP 5 — PRESERVE NORMAL BEHAVIOR

If trade control is NOT active:

Use real market price and calculatePnL normally.

---

XAU/USD BUY
Entry = 5513.92

Effective price = 5514.92

User sees:

~+$11 profit

regardless of current bid price.
